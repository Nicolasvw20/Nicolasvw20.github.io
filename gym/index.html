Progression rules

Simple and rigorous
Main lifts: finish most sets with around two reps in reserve. Reach the top of the rep range across all sets, then add the smallest practical load.

Unilateral legs: weaker side first; match its reps on the stronger side. Do not add failure sets.

Deload: halve working sets and keep 3–4 reps in reserve on the scheduled deload week.

Symptoms: stop sharp pain. Reduce the following session if gait, swelling or joint pain is clearly worse later that day or next morning.
